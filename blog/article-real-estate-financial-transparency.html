Frequently Asked Questions

How can mortgage calculators help close real estate deals?

When a prospect can see instantly that their dream home costs $2,100/month rather than an abstract '$480,000,' the decision becomes real and personal. Sharing a Feexio mortgage calculator link in a WhatsApp message or email lets clients run their own numbers in real time, which reduces hesitation and builds trust. Financial transparency is a competitive advantage in real estate.

What is the best way to share calculators with real estate clients?

Send a direct link to the specific calculator with a brief note: 'Here's a calculator to estimate your monthly payment for the property we discussed.' WhatsApp links (generated with tools like InstantLinkHub) work well for initial outreach. For formal follow-up, include the calculator link in a PDF proposal alongside estimated numbers. The goal is to remove friction between 'interested' and 'ready to offer.'

How accurate are online mortgage calculators?

Online calculators provide reliable estimates for principal and interest. They do not include property taxes, homeowner's insurance, HOA fees, or PMI — all of which can add $300–$700/month to the true cost of ownership. Always present calculator results alongside a clear note that taxes, insurance, and other costs are not included. Transparency about what's estimated versus what's exact builds more trust than presenting overly precise numbers.
