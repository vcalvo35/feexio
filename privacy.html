Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

1. Overview

Feexio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website feexio.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and free financial calculator tools.

We are committed to your privacy. By design, our calculators process all inputs locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the numerical inputs you enter into our calculators to any server.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect:

  • Calculator inputs (amounts, prices, rates, etc.)
  • Personal financial information
  • Account information (we have no accounts)

We may collect (automatically, via analytics):

  • Pages visited and time spent on site (anonymized)
  • Referring URL and browser/device type
  • General geographic location (country/region level, not precise)
  • IP address (used for fraud prevention and analytics, not stored long-term)

3. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to:

  • Remember your dark mode preference (localStorage)
  • Remember your language preference (localStorage)
  • Serve display advertisements via third-party ad networks (Google AdSense or similar)

Third-party advertising partners may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising through the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized site traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — display advertising
  • Google Fonts — typography (font loading may create a connection to Google servers)

Each third party has its own privacy policy governing data use.

5. Data Retention

We do not store your calculator inputs at any point. Analytics data is retained in aggregated form for up to 26 months, after which it is deleted. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months for correspondence purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@feexio.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Feexio is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@feexio.com
