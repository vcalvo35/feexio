Why Feexio Exists

There's a specific kind of frustration that hits when you've just quoted a client $800, sent the PayPal invoice, and the money that arrives is $773.80. You knew there'd be fees. You just didn't know exactly how much — and now you're not sure if you actually charged enough to cover your time, your costs, and your taxes.

That gap between "I think this is profitable" and "I know this is profitable" is what Feexio was built to close. We started as a set of personal spreadsheets — rate calculators, fee tables, margin formulas — that got shared with freelance friends, refined through real use, and eventually rebuilt as a proper tool set that anyone can use in seconds, for free.

The tools are deliberately simple. No accounts to create. No data stored. No premium tier with the features you actually need locked behind a paywall. You put your numbers in, you get your answer, and you move on with your day smarter than when you arrived.

All fee data is reviewed and verified periodically. When platform fee structures change — and they do — we update the calculators. Every page that carries fee data shows a "Last verified" date so you know exactly how current the information is.

✍️ The Author

About Victor A. Calvo S.

👤

Victor A. Calvo S. is a software engineer and digital entrepreneur who builds practical, free tools for independent workers, freelancers, and small businesses. He is the creator of Feexio, InstantLinkHub, and SwiftConvertHub — three open-access platforms focused on fee calculation, link generation, and unit conversion respectively.

His tools cover the practical side of running an independent business: payment platform fees (PayPal, Stripe, Fiverr), profit margin analysis, freelance rate calculation, and loan comparisons. Victor writes plainly about numbers — no jargon, no generic advice, just the math and what it means for your bottom line.

Victor built the first version of Feexio after spending too many hours answering the same fee and pricing questions in freelance communities. He believed every independent worker deserved the same instant clarity that accountants and finance teams provide to larger companies — and that it shouldn't cost anything to get it.

Victor writes all editorial content on Feexio. Fee data is verified by cross-referencing official platform documentation. Content is reviewed and updated on a regular schedule.

Our Mission

The global freelance economy is massive — and growing. But too many independent workers make financial decisions based on guesswork. Every freelancer, seller, and entrepreneur deserves the same clarity that big companies have: instant access to accurate numbers, without needing a finance team.
