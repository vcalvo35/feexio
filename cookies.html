Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How Feexio Uses Cookies

Feexio uses a minimal number of cookies strictly necessary for functionality and analytics. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites or build advertising profiles.

Essential / Functional Cookies

Name Purpose Duration
feexio-theme Remembers your light/dark mode preference Persistent (localStorage)

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use Feexio in aggregate — which tools are most useful, which pages are visited, and how to improve the site. Analytics data is anonymized and never linked to personally identifiable information.

Name Purpose Duration
_ga Distinguishes users (anonymized) 2 years
_ga_* Maintains session state for analytics 2 years

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

Feexio displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out at any time (see section 4 below).

Name Purpose Provider
IDE, DSID Ad personalization and measurement Google

3. What We Do NOT Use

  • We do not use any third-party ad networks other than Google AdSense.
  • We do not sell or share cookie data with data brokers.
  • We do not use cookies to collect your calculator inputs or financial information.
  • All calculator computations happen locally in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via their privacy/security settings. See your browser's help pages for instructions.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Google Ads personalization: Manage your preferences at adssettings.google.com.
  • General ad opt-out: Visit optout.aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.eu (EU).

Note: disabling cookies may affect your experience (e.g., your light/dark theme preference won't be remembered).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we change the tools or services we use. The "last updated" date at the top reflects any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
